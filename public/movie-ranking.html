Mikiko's Favorite Movies

My top three movies of all time.

1. Pride and Prejudice (2009)

This is a historical fiction romance. It is based off my favorite book. This is the best version of the film.

2. Lord of the Rings: Two Towers

This movie is the best of the Lord of the Rings series because of the ents. I love those awesome tree dudes. One day they will find where the tree ladies went.

3. Coco

This is a animated movie about the day of the dead in Mexico. It is so sweet and makes me cry every time.
